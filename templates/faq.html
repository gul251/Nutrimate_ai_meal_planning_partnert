FAQ

Quick answers about accounts, AI suggestions, and data.

1

How does NutriMate create meal plans?

NutriMate uses your profile (age, weight, height, activity, diet type) and goal to generate personalised suggestions. When AI quota is exhausted, local fallback suggestions are used automatically.

2

Is NutriMate a medical app?

No. NutriMate is for general wellness and educational planning only. For medical conditions or clinical dietary requirements, please consult a qualified professional.

3

Why am I seeing fallback-only suggestions?

This typically means the AI provider's quota is temporarily exhausted. Your saved meals and local suggestions still work normally.

4

Where is my data stored?

Your profile and meal data are stored in Firebase Firestore with authenticated access. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

5

How do I delete my account or data?

Send a request to nutrimte.test@gmail.com and we will process it promptly.

6

How do I contact support?

Visit the Contact page or email nutrimte.test@gmail.com directly.
