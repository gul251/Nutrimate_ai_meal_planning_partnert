Privacy Policy

How NutriMate collects, uses, and protects your information.

1

Information We Collect

We collect account details (name, email), profile data (age, weight, height, diet, activity level), meal entries, and progress logs you enter inside the app.

2

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to personalise meal suggestions, calculate nutrition targets, and display your progress. We do not sell or share data with third parties.

3

Storage & Security

All data is stored in Firebase Firestore with authenticated access controls. Data is transmitted over HTTPS only.

4

AI Processing

When AI suggestions are active, your profile details are sent to our AI provider through a secure backend route. If AI is unavailable, local fallback suggestions are used instead.

5

Your Rights

You can update your profile at any time. For account or full data deletion requests, contact nutrimte.test@gmail.com.

6

Policy Updates

We may update this policy as the project evolves. Significant changes will appear on this page with an updated date.
