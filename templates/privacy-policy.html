Privacy Policy

How NutriMate collects, uses, and protects your information.

1

Information We Collect

We collect your account details (name, email), profile data (age, weight, height, diet, activity), meal entries, and progress logs that you submit inside the app.

2

How We Use Your Data

We use this data to personalize meal suggestions, calculate goals, and show your progress. Data is used only to provide app features and improve your experience.

3

Storage and Security

Your app data is stored in Firebase services with authenticated access controls. We use HTTPS communication for secure data transfer between your browser and backend services.

4

AI Processing

When AI suggestions are enabled, profile details required for suggestions may be sent to our AI provider through secure backend routes. If AI is unavailable, local fallback suggestions are used.

5

Your Control

You can update profile data at any time. For account/data removal requests, contact us at nutrimte.test@gmail.com.

6

Policy Updates

We may update this policy as the project evolves. Significant changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
