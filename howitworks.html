How NutriMate Works

Simple steps to plan meals and track your health easily

1

Create Your Profile

Enter your basic details such as age, height, weight, activity level, and diet preference. This helps NutriMate understand your body needs.

2

Analyze Health Data

NutriMate analyzes your information to calculate calories, protein needs, and overall nutrition requirements.

3

Get Personalized Meal Plans

Based on your goals, NutriMate suggests daily meal plans that match your calorie and diet targets.

4

Track Daily Meals

Add your meals for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snacks and track calories, protein, and cost in one place.

5

Monitor Progress

Record your weight and view progress over time to stay motivated and consistent with your health goals.
