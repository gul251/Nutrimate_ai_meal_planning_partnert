Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about account setup, AI suggestions, and data usage.

1

How does NutriMate create meal plans?

NutriMate uses your profile, diet type, and goals to generate suggestions. When AI is temporarily unavailable, the app uses local fallback suggestions so planning does not stop.

2

Is this a medical app?

No. NutriMate is designed for educational and wellness planning support. For medical conditions, consult a qualified health professional.

3

Why am I seeing fallback-only suggestions?

This usually happens when AI provider quota is exhausted or the provider is temporarily unavailable. You can still continue with local suggestions and saved meals.

4

Where is my data stored?

Profile and meal data are stored in Firebase services with authenticated access. See our Privacy Policy for details.

5

How do I contact support?

Send your question to nutrimte.test@gmail.com or visit the Contact page.
